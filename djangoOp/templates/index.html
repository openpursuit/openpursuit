Welcome in OpenPursuit

The first free and "open" database of question and answer, all released under Creative Commons licence.

  • How does it works? It's easy. Everyone can register to the site and then populate a free database. Each question/answer matches one or more tags so it is possibile to play with friend with questions related to generic topics like "art" or "literature", or very specific ones such "internet", "math", "geeks". Everyone can use the database by plaing in this site, in othersite or dump some question and play it offline, for example on a mobile phone. More detailated info in the instructions page.

  • What do you mean with "Open"? The OpenPursuit project follow an open architecture. Open source server side code released under GPL3 licence, quiz released under creative commons open licences, database open for input and output insert/queries, a community open to accept you in the staff for discussuss on the project and control the quality of the quiz.

  • Can I partecipate? Sure! You can partecipate adding new questions, joining the mailing list and eventually enter in the openpursuit developing team
  • What else? Dunno! Just play and enjoy yourself! :-)
